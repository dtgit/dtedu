This is a list of terms and their meaning in a Kupu context.
If you know any of the following to be wrong, or would like to see a
definition for a particular term, please notify
kupu-dev@codespeak.net! 

toolbar

  This is the bar of buttons at the top of a Kupu editing template.

toolbox
 
  These appear on the right of the Kupu editing template.

drawer

  A type of pop-up window that appears when you click on certain
  buttons in the toolbar.
